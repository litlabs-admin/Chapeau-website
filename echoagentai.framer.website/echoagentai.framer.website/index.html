Everything in sync

One customer view

Stop piecing together emails, notes, and Slack threads. Echo brings every interaction into a single timeline so you can see the full story before you reply, call, or make a decision.

Clear next step

No more staring at a pipeline wondering what to do. Echo looks at activity, momentum, and past outcomes, then suggests the next best move so you can act instead of guess.

Forecasts you can stand behind

Static spreadsheets go stale fast. Echo updates your forecast as deals move, stall, or speed up, giving you a live view of what is likely to close and what needs attention.

Automatic CRM updates

Echo logs meetings, captures emails, and summarizes calls in the background. Your CRM stays accurate without the end-of-week data cleanup.

Relationship signals

See who is engaged, who has gone quiet, and where risk is building. Echo highlights shifts in response patterns and buying signals before they turn into lost deals.
